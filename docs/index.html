How does spaced repetition help me pass the NCLEX?

Spaced repetition is a scientifically-proven study technique that shows you flashcards at increasing intervals based on how well you know them. Cards you struggle with appear more frequently, while mastered cards appear less often. This optimizes your study time and improves long-term retention of nursing concepts — critical for passing the NCLEX on your first attempt.

Topics include med-surg, pharmacology, pediatrics, maternity/OB, mental health, leadership, fundamentals, and safety.
