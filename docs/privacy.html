CozyNCLEX Prep Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 25, 2026

Summary: CozyNCLEX Prep collects your email address to create an account and syncs your study progress to the cloud so you can access it across devices. We do not sell your data or share it with advertisers.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address – Required to create and secure your account
  • Display name – Optional, used to personalize your experience
  • Authentication data – If you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive a unique identifier and email from those services

Study Data

We collect and sync the following study-related data:

  • Study progress and statistics (cards studied, accuracy rates)
  • Mastered cards and mastery streaks
  • XP, level, and achievements
  • Daily streaks and study goals
  • Quiz and test scores
  • Custom flashcards you create
  • Card notes and saved/flagged cards

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Location data
  • Contacts or phone data
  • Health or biometric data
  • Payment information (handled entirely by Apple)
  • Browsing history outside the app

2. How We Use Your Information

Data Purpose
Email address Account creation, password reset, important account notifications
Study progress Sync across your devices, track your learning, personalize study recommendations
Subscription status Provide access to premium features

3. Data Storage and Security

Where Your Data Is Stored

  • Locally on your device – For offline access and fast performance
  • Supabase cloud servers – For account data and cross-device sync (servers located in the United States)
  • Apple iCloud – Optional sync via CloudKit if enabled

Security Measures

We protect your data using:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure authentication via Supabase Auth
  • Row-level security policies on our database
  • Apple's built-in iOS security features

5. Your Rights and Choices

You Can:

  • Access your data – View your study statistics and progress in the app
  • Delete your account – Contact us to permanently delete your account and all associated data
  • Disable cloud sync – Use iCloud sync settings to control what syncs
  • Sign out – Sign out of your account at any time in Settings

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal purposes.

6. Children's Privacy

CozyNCLEX Prep is intended for users who are at least 17 years old (nursing students and professionals). We do not knowingly collect information from children under 17. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy in the app or via email. Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
