Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 22, 2026

Summary: CozyNCLEX Prep respects your privacy. We collect minimal data, store everything locally on your device, and never sell your information.

1. Information We Collect

Data Stored Locally on Your Device:

  • Study progress and statistics
  • Quiz scores and performance history
  • Custom flashcards you create
  • App preferences and settings
  • Achievement progress

This data is stored only on your device using Apple's secure storage and is not transmitted to our servers.

2. Subscription Information

If you purchase a subscription:

  • Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store
  • We do not collect, store, or have access to your payment information
  • Apple may share your purchase history with us for subscription verification purposes

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, address)
  • Location data
  • Contacts or phone data
  • Photos or media
  • Browsing history
  • Payment or financial information

4. Analytics

We may use Apple's built-in App Analytics to understand general app usage patterns. This data is anonymized and aggregated. You can opt out of sharing analytics with developers in your device's Privacy settings.

5. Push Notifications

If you enable study reminders, notification data is processed locally on your device. We do not have access to your notification preferences or history.

6. Data Security

Your study data is stored securely on your device using iOS's built-in data protection. Since we don't transmit your data to external servers, your information remains under your control.

7. Children's Privacy

CozyNCLEX Prep is intended for nursing students and healthcare professionals preparing for the NCLEX examination. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

8. Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by:

  • Deleting the app from your device
  • Using the "Reset Progress" option in Settings (if available)

9. Third-Party Services

The app integrates with:

  • Apple StoreKit: For subscription management
  • Apple Push Notification Service: For study reminders (optional)

These services are governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

11. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including:

  • Right to access your data
  • Right to delete your data
  • Right to opt out of analytics
