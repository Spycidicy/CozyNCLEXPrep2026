Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchases?

Go to Settings > Subscription > Restore Purchases. This will restore any previous premium subscriptions associated with your Apple ID.

How does the mastery system work?

Cards become "mastered" after you answer them correctly 3 times in a row. Mastered cards won't appear in study modes, but you can always review them in the Card Browser.

What is Smart Review?

Smart Review uses spaced repetition to show you cards at optimal intervals for memory retention. Cards you struggle with appear more frequently.

How does the CAT Simulation work?

Like the real NCLEX, our CAT Simulation adapts to your performance. Answer correctly and questions get harder; answer incorrectly and they get easier. The test can end early if you demonstrate clear competency.

How do I reset my progress?

Go to Settings > Data > Reset All Progress. Warning: This cannot be undone and will delete all your study data.

Study Tips

  • Study a little every day - consistency beats cramming
  • Use the Daily Goals feature to stay on track
  • Review rationales carefully, even for correct answers
  • Focus on weak categories shown in your Stats
  • Take practice tests regularly to simulate exam conditions
